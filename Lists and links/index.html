Some Interesting Facts about Film History :)

Ordered List +Linking to a Specific Part of the Same Page

  1. Originally, the term “movies” did not mean films, but the people who made them.
  2. The first moving picture cameras were invented towards the late 1800s, and movies were boring. They were a single scene, about a minute long, and they were silent.
  3. To make up for the lack of sound in the film, a band would play live music while the movie ran.
  4. 13 FRAMES PER SECOND - The minimum speed that the human brain needs in order to process consecutive images as movement. Anything less than that, the human brain will process each frame as a separate picture. 16 is pretty close to 13, which is why old movies look so choppy and unnatural.

Unordered List + Opening Link in a New Window
